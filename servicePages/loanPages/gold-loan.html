FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about gold loans

How is my gold valued for the loan?

Your gold is evaluated based on purity (karat), weight, and the prevailing market gold rate using standardized testing methods.

Is my gold safe with you?

Yes. Your gold is stored in insured, high-security vaults with 24/7 surveillance and strict access controls.

Can I get my gold back anytime?

Absolutely. You can repay the loan anytime and retrieve your gold with no prepayment or foreclosure charges.

What happens if I can't repay on time?

We provide reminders and flexible repayment options. If repayment is delayed beyond the tenure, applicable penalties may apply.

Do you accept gold with stones?

Yes, gold jewellery with stones is accepted. However, only the gold weight is considered for valuation.
