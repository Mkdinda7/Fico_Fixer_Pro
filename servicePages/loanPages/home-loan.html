Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about home loans

What is the maximum home loan amount I can get?
The maximum loan amount depends on your income, credit score, repayment capacity, and property value. Typically, you can get up to 75‚Äì90% of the property value.
What are the current home loan interest rates?
Home loan interest rates usually start from 8.50% p.a. and may vary based on applicant profile, loan amount, and tenure.
How long does the home loan approval take?
Loan approval generally takes 24 hours to 7 working days, depending on document verification and credit assessment.
Can I prepay my home loan?
Yes, you can prepay your home loan partially or fully. Most lenders allow prepayment without charges on floating-rate loans.
What tax benefits can I avail on home loan?
You can claim tax deductions under Section 80C on principal repayment and Section 24(b) on interest paid, subject to prevailing tax laws.
