Got Questions?

Questions & Answers

Find answers to commonly asked questions about NRI banking

What is an NRI Account?

An NRI account is a bank account designed for Non-Resident Indians to manage income earned in India and abroad.

Can I open an NRI account online?

Yes, you can open an NRI account completely online with digital documentation and verification.

What is the difference between NRE and NRO accounts?

NRE accounts hold foreign income and are tax-free, while NRO accounts manage income earned in India and are taxable.

Is my NRI account deposit safe?

Yes, all NRI deposits are RBI regulated and protected with bank-grade security protocols.

Can I transfer money from NRE to NRO account?

Yes, transfers from NRE to NRO accounts are allowed as per RBI guidelines.

What are the minimum balance requirements?

Minimum balance requirements vary by account type and will be communicated during onboarding.

How can I access my account from abroad?

You can access your account anytime using our mobile app and internet banking services worldwide.

What happens to my NRI account if I return to India?

On returning to India, your NRI account must be converted to a resident account as per RBI norms.
