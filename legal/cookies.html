Cookies Policy

Learn how FICO Fixer Pro uses cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us enhance your experience, analyze traffic, and personalize services.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • To remember your login session
  • To analyze website traffic and performance
  • To personalize content and recommendations
  • To improve platform functionality

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand user behavior.
  • Analytics Cookies: Improve features and usability.
  • Marketing Cookies: Deliver relevant advertisements.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

Tip: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies under Privacy Settings.

5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology or legal requirements. Continued use of our website indicates your acceptance of these updates.
